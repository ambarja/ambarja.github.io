About Me

Welcome to my portfolio webpage/blog. Over the last 6+ years I have been working with MS equipment including the high resolution Q-ToFs as well as triple quadrupole MS, coupled with various liquid chromatography setups such as from micro- and nano-LCs.

I develop workflows for analysis and quantification of complex biological samples by making use of techniques including but not limited to HILIC chromatography, RPLC, and use of stable isotopes for development and analysis of a wide range of assays. Check out this page to know more about my skills, research and research experience and my education background.

Connect with me on Linkedin or Twitter to say Hi, or you can also contact me directly through my email.
